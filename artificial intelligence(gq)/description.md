## Subject: Artificial Intelligence (AI)

### Course Description

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the field of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**, the discipline focused on creating intelligent agents and systems that can perceive their environment, reason, learn, and act autonomously.

We will explore the fundamental principles and techniques that form the basis of modern AI. The course covers the two major pillars: **symbolic AI** (based on logic and search) and **connectionist AI** (based on data and machine learning). Students will gain an understanding of how to build systems that can solve complex problems, make decisions under uncertainty, and learn from experience.
