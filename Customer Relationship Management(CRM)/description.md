## Subject: Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

### Course Description

This course provides a comprehensive overview of **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)**, a vital strategy for modern businesses. We will explore CRM as both a business philosophy and a technology-driven toolset designed to manage and nurture a company's interactions with its current and potential customers.

The primary goal of CRM is to improve business relationships, driving customer loyalty, retention, and sales growth. This subject covers the principles behind customer-centric strategies, the operational and analytical components of CRM, and the practical application of CRM systems to achieve business objectives.
